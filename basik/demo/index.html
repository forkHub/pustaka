BASIK

Basik is a lightweight library designed to make learning and working with the canvas easier and more approachable. Whether you're starting a small app or prototyping a game, it helps you get things up and running quickly. Basik draws inspiration from Blitz Basic and p5.js, aiming to keep things simple, flexible, and fun. Students, Teacher, Educators, Programmers are welcome.
